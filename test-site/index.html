Google

Google is the world's biggest internet search provider. It is so successful because it incoporates many other services such as images, maps, translate etc.

  • 'Google' is a play on the mathematical term 'googol' which is a 1 with a hundred zeros after it.
  • As of October 2018, Google owned 90.28% of the world market share of search engines.
  • Since 2010 Google has been buying one company every week on average. They own Youtube, Android, Waze, etc.
